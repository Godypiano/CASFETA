Out-reach Mission

To reach the area where the gospel of Christ does not Spread at great extent or not at all.We execute two times per one academic year.

In-reach Mission

This mission done within our campus area and the main purpose is to preach the gospel of Christ to our fellow students.

Door-to-Door Mission

The main target is to shares good news to single person,we execute within our campus on sunday after church services.

Church Visitation

The main purpose is to build good relationship with neighborhood churches where our members regularly attend for worshiping.

Charity Visitation

This mission is for people who are in special needs like prisoners,street children,widows,ophans,disabled people ets.

Education Motivation

We motivate and develop habit of learning to our members and non-members from primary to university through EMC organization

Casfeta Tayomi

CASFETA is a Pentecostal students association in Secondary Schools,Colleges and Universities in Tanzania.

CASFETA operates under the Trusteeship of TANZANIA YOUTH MINISTRIES(TAYOMI). TAYOMI is a registered organization under the Ministry of Home Affairs in the United Republic of Tanzania. Our Vision is "Change the Life of the Youth to Change the World,I First Then Others".
